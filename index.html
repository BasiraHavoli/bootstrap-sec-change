About Me

My name is Basira Havoli, a passionate 20-year-old Industrial Engineering student at the University of Ghent. I'm driven and eager to learn and grow. 

As an Industrial Engineering student, I am committed to understanding the intricacies of the field and finding innovative solutions to complex problems.
